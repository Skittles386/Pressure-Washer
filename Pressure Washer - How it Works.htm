When we were working on the pressure washer, we took off the ignition coil. The way our ignition coil works
is by using a flywheel which has a small magnetic plate which passes a point to create an electrical charge to ignite the spark
plug. The flywheel turns by pulling a cord that is connected to a plastic piece that will turn the flywheel and create a spark in the piston
chamber. After the spark of the piston chamber goes off, it will ignite the fuel and produce an explosion that moves
the camshaft. The camshaft powers whatever it is attached to, making the components spin and work.
