The main cause of a small engine failure is the carburetor, by leftover fuel in the jet
and the bowl of the carburetor which can cause it to clog up over time if not used. To prevent this from happening,
it is used to either start or run for 10 to 15 minutes a day. When you go to store away a small engine, (for
longer than 2 months) make sure there is no gas in the fuel lines and carburetor to prevent all of the
internals from getting clogged by expired gas.
